JobTailor

Upload your resume, find USA tech jobs (remote or in your state), and let AI read your whole resume to build a polished, tailored resume + cover letter for each. Runs on your computer.

⚙️ Set up once — AI engine & job-site key

Keys are remembered on this computer and only used by you. AI is what reads your whole resume and writes the polished documents.

AI engine

From console.anthropic.com → API keys (separate from your Claude.ai subscription).

Free at aistudio.google.com → Get API key.

Big job sites — Indeed, LinkedIn, ZipRecruiter, Glassdoor

Free at rapidapi.com → search JSearch → Subscribe to Basic (Free) → copy the X-RapidAPI-Key.

More job sources (optional, free)

Adzuna adds all-industry US listings; USAJobs adds federal jobs. Note: these APIs are built for servers, so a browser may block them (Adzuna sometimes works; USAJobs usually needs a helper). If a source fails, the “Why:” line under your results shows the exact reason.

✓ Saved

Keys live only in this browser — they are never part of the shared file. Use Clear saved keys before sharing your screen or a computer.
